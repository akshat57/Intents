Re-recording in Second Language

Instructions:

Please re-record your previous messages in your selected languages.

  1. Your previous recordings will be displayed one by one. Please play them again for reference by clicking the play button.
  2. The context of the recordings is also given.
  3. Then press 'Record' to record the translation of the audio in your chosen language.
  4. Press 'Stop' when you're done recording.
  5. Press I HAVE READ THE INSTRUCTIONS to begin.
