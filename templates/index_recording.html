Please start a conversation with the Bank


Instructions :

  • The Bank will ask you questions.
  • Press 'Reply' to begin recording your reply.
  • Press 'Stop' to stop recording.
  • You can listen to your recording to check if audio was properly record. You can record again in case of errors.
  • If you think your recording sounds okay, press 'Send to Bank'.
  • The bank will respond by asking a question, please respond appropriately.
  • Only after your conversation is finished, press 'yes'.

What should I ask the Bank?

Please ask the app about any ONE of the following:
  • Ask to send money to someone
  • Ask about your account balance
  • Ask about your last transaction
  • Ask to withdraw money
  • Ask to deposit money
